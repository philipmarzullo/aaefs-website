MEP Services

Preventive maintenance, emergency response, and building systems management

MECHANICAL, ELECTRICAL & PLUMBING

Keep Your Building Running — Not Reacting

A&A's MEP team manages the mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems that keep your facility operational. Our approach is preventive by design: scheduled maintenance, asset tracking through TMA (our CMMS platform), and building management system (BMS) integration that catches issues before they become emergencies.

When emergencies do happen, our response teams are trained, equipped, and on-call. From HVAC failures to plumbing breaks to electrical faults, we respond fast and document everything — so you have a clear record for compliance and planning.

As part of A&A's integrated service model, MEP operates alongside janitorial and grounds under single-point accountability. That means one partner managing your entire facility — no gaps, no handoff confusion, and one team that knows your building inside and out.

MEP Capabilities

Preventive Maintenance
Scheduled PM routines for HVAC, electrical, plumbing, and fire protection systems — tracked through TMA with compliance documentation, asset histories, and automated scheduling to maximize equipment uptime.
Emergency Response
On-call teams ready for HVAC failures, plumbing emergencies, electrical faults, and equipment breakdowns. Every call is logged, every response is timed, and every resolution is documented for your records.
Building Systems Management
BMS integration, energy management, and systems monitoring for complex facilities. Our technicians work with your building automation platforms to optimize performance, reduce energy costs, and maintain occupant comfort.
Asset Management (TMA)
Full CMMS integration through TMA for PM scheduling, work order management, compliance tracking, and asset uptime reporting. Every piece of equipment has a documented history, maintenance schedule, and performance record.

SPECIALIZED TRAINING

Certified, Qualified, Ready

A&A's MEP technicians go through specialized training tracks that go well beyond basic maintenance. Our certification programs are built for the real demands of complex building environments.

  • Lockout/Tagout — OSHA-compliant energy isolation procedures for every service call
  • Confined Space Entry — Certified protocols for mechanical rooms, tunnels, and restricted areas
  • Arc Flash Safety — Electrical hazard awareness and protection for panel and switchgear work
  • BMS Integration — Training on building automation systems to support monitoring, diagnostics, and optimization
  • Preventive Maintenance Routines — Standardized PM procedures tracked and verified through TMA

All certifications are tracked through AA360, with annual refresher training and monthly safety performance reviews.
