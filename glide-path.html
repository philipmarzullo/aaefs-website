Glide Path Partnership

Shared savings that align our success with yours

PARTNERSHIP MODEL

When We Save You Money, We Both Win

Glide Path is A&A's shared-savings partnership model. The concept is straightforward: we identify and implement efficiency gains in your facility operations, those gains are independently verified, and a percentage of the savings is returned directly to you.

This creates a built-in incentive for A&A to continuously find ways to reduce your costs — not by cutting corners, but by operating smarter. Better scheduling, reduced waste, optimized staffing, and technology-driven improvements all contribute to savings that benefit both sides.

Governance oversight remains under full client authority. You approve every initiative, verify every result, and control the pace of change. Glide Path only works when both partners trust the process.

HOW IT WORKS

The Glide Path Cycle

Glide Path follows a repeatable four-step cycle. Each round builds on the previous one, creating compounding efficiency gains over the life of the partnership.

1
Baseline Assessment
We document your current operational costs, staffing levels, equipment usage, supply consumption, and service delivery patterns. This establishes the measurable baseline that all future savings are calculated against.
2
Efficiency Identification
Our operations team analyzes every workflow, schedule, and process to identify where waste exists and where smarter approaches can reduce cost without reducing quality. We present recommendations with projected savings for your review.
3
Implementation
Approved initiatives are rolled out with clear timelines, assigned ownership, and measurable targets. We track every change against the baseline to ensure gains are real and sustainable — not one-time adjustments.
4
Verified Savings Shared
Efficiency gains are independently verified against the original baseline. A percentage of confirmed savings is returned to you. Then the cycle repeats — new baseline, new opportunities, compounding returns year over year.

WHY GLIDE PATH WORKS

Incentives Aligned at Every Level

Cost Reduction
Real, measurable savings returned to your bottom line — not theoretical projections but verified results you can see in your operating budget.
Transparency
Every initiative, every metric, every dollar is documented and visible. You approve changes before they happen and verify results after they're delivered.
Aligned Incentives
When A&A saves you money, we both benefit. That means every team member from the account manager to the floor specialist is motivated to find smarter ways to operate.
Continuous Improvement
Glide Path isn't a one-time exercise. Each cycle establishes a new baseline and uncovers new opportunities, creating compounding value over the life of the partnership.

Glide Path turns your facility services contract into a partnership that gets better every year. If you want to see how shared savings could work for your operation, let's run the numbers together.
