Industries We Serve

Facility Expertise Across Sectors

Every industry has its own standards, compliance requirements, and operational rhythms. A&A brings 50+ years of experience to each environment we serve — from Class A office towers and university campuses to hospitals, government buildings, and manufacturing floors. Our integrated model (janitorial, grounds, and MEP under one accountability structure) adapts to meet the specific demands of your sector while delivering the performance metrics our clients have come to expect.

We don't offer a one-size-fits-all program. We build service plans around the realities of your environment, then prove performance through documented results and real-time reporting via AA360.

Commercial Office Space Tenant satisfaction, lobby presentation, and building systems
Healthcare Infection control, compliance, and patient environment quality
Education Campus-wide services from classrooms to residence halls
Life Sciences Controlled environments, cleanrooms, and compliance documentation
Industrial Manufacturing floors, warehouse care, and safety compliance
Government Security-sensitive facilities with full MBE/MWBE certification
Hospitality Guest experience, event support, and presentation standards
Aviation Terminal environments, high-traffic care, and 24/7 operations

Your Industry. Our Expertise.
