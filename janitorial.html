Janitorial Services

Daily cleaning, deep clean programs, health protocols, and event support

THE A&A APPROACH

Cleaning Built on Systems, Not Guesswork

A&A's janitorial services are powered by our SYNC task-based service model, which assigns five specialist roles to every account for clarity and accountability. Every task is tracked. Every shift is verified. Every expectation is documented and measurable.

Our teams are trained to APPA-level standards with visual task cards and standard operating procedures in every required language. Lighthouse, our real-time task completion platform, gives facility managers live visibility into what's been done, what's in progress, and what's coming next.

From daily cleaning to specialized deep cleans and health protocols, we bring the same rigor to a classroom as we do to a clinical lab. Green cleaning certification is standard across our janitorial workforce, and AA360 tracks every QA walkthrough, inspection score, and corrective action.

Janitorial Capabilities

Daily Cleaning
Routine custodial services managed through SYNC's five-role model — from lobby care and restroom sanitation to office cleaning and trash removal. Every task is assigned, tracked, and verified through Lighthouse in real time.
Deep Clean Programs
Scheduled project-level cleaning that goes beyond daily maintenance — hard floor restoration, carpet extraction, high-touch surface disinfection, and terminal cleans. Planned around your operational calendar to minimize disruption.
Health & Safety Protocols
Infection control procedures, enhanced disinfection programs, and green cleaning protocols certified to industry standards. Our teams are trained in bloodborne pathogen cleanup, hazard communication, and PPE best practices.
Event Support
Pre-event setup, during-event maintenance, and post-event restoration for conferences, commencements, galas, and campus events. We staff to the occasion and scale on demand without compromising daily service levels.

Our Quality Assurance Cycle

Every janitorial account runs on A&A's five-phase QA cycle — a continuous loop that turns data into action and action into results.

1

Planning

Site evaluation, customized cleaning plan, standardized procedures, and resource allocation — staffing, equipment, and supplies mapped to your facility's specific needs.

2

Deployment

Training and certification, implementation of standardized procedures, and direct coordination with your facility management team to align on priorities from day one.

3

Data Visualization & Analysis

Routine inspections and quality audits generate real-time KPIs — cleanliness ratings, incident reports, satisfaction scores — presented in visual reports through AA360 with built-in feedback mechanisms.

4

Action Plans

Trend analysis and root cause identification drive targeted interventions. Resources are reallocated as needed, and continuous monitoring ensures corrective actions stick.

5

Driving Results

Performance reviews, KPI analysis, ongoing training, and process refinement — with transparent reporting and regular stakeholder engagement to keep everyone aligned.
