Industrial

Safe, Clean Facilities Keep Operations Running

Industrial environments demand facility teams who understand safety compliance, heavy-use floor care, and the operational realities of manufacturing and warehouse spaces. A&A provides trained teams with OSHA and HAZMAT awareness, full PPE compliance, and the discipline to maintain consistent standards in demanding conditions.

Our manager-heavy model means on-site accountability every shift. Combined with SYNC's structured task assignments and AA360's real-time tracking, you get documented proof that safety and cleaning protocols are being followed — not just assumed.

Capabilities for Industrial

Industrial Floor Care
Heavy-duty floor cleaning, scrubbing, and maintenance for manufacturing floors, warehouse spaces, and loading areas. We use industrial-grade equipment and techniques designed for high-traffic, high-soil environments.
Safety Protocol Implementation
Our teams are trained in OSHA compliance, HAZMAT awareness, and proper PPE usage. We implement and maintain safety protocols specific to your facility, with documented training records accessible through AA360.
Equipment Area Maintenance
Cleaning and maintenance around production equipment, conveyor systems, and assembly areas. Our SYNC model ensures designated specialists handle sensitive equipment zones with the care and protocols they require.
Waste Management
Structured waste collection, sorting, and removal programs for industrial facilities. We coordinate with your operations team to minimize disruption while maintaining clean, organized workspaces and compliant disposal practices.
