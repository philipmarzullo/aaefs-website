Grounds Maintenance

Landscaping, snow removal, irrigation, and athletic field management

YEAR-ROUND CURB APPEAL

Your Grounds Are the First Impression

The outside of your facility sets expectations before anyone walks through the door. A&A's grounds maintenance team delivers year-round care — from spring landscaping through winter snow and ice management — with the same accountability and tracking that defines our indoor services.

Our crews are trained in seasonal task readiness, equipment safety, and turf robot orientation. Whether you manage a corporate campus, a university quad, or an athletic complex, we build a grounds plan around your calendar, your climate, and your priorities.

As part of A&A's integrated service model, grounds maintenance operates alongside janitorial and MEP under single-point accountability. That means your outdoor spaces get the same attention, data tracking, and QA oversight as every square foot inside your building.

Grounds Capabilities

Landscaping
Full-service landscape management including mowing, edging, pruning, mulching, seasonal color rotations, irrigation system management, and turf care. We plan around your growing season and build maintenance schedules that keep your property looking sharp year-round.
Snow & Ice Removal
Pre-storm preparation, plowing, salting, de-icing, and walkway clearing — with response plans calibrated to your facility's traffic patterns and safety requirements. We monitor weather conditions and mobilize before the first flake hits the ground.
Athletic Field Management
Turf care, field striping, irrigation scheduling, and seasonal preparation for athletic complexes. We maintain playing surfaces to competition standards and coordinate maintenance windows around game schedules and event calendars.
Autonomous Mowing
Robotic mowing technology for consistent turf maintenance with reduced labor costs and environmental impact. Our teams are trained in turf robot orientation, programming, and oversight — technology that supports crews, not replaces them.

Ready to Talk Performance?

Your facility's exterior is the first thing visitors, tenants, and employees see every day. Let's talk about how A&A's grounds team can keep your property looking its best — every season, every day.
