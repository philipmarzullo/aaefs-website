AA360 Technology Platform

Real-time visibility into your facility's performance

TECHNOLOGY PLATFORM

Technology That Proves Performance — Not Just Promises It

AA360 is A&A's integrated technology platform that brings together quality auditing, multilingual training, performance analytics, robotics integration, AI-powered auditing, and space utilization into a single system.

Instead of relying on reports that tell you what happened last month, AA360 gives you real-time data on what's happening right now. Every inspection, every task completion, every training milestone is captured and accessible — so you can verify performance with evidence, not assumptions.

AA360 integrates with TMA for asset management, Lighthouse for task tracking, Corrigo, and your existing client systems to create a unified view of facility operations.

PLATFORM CAPABILITIES

What AA360 Delivers

QA Tracking & Auditing
Scheduled and ad-hoc quality audits captured digitally with photo documentation, scoring, and trend analysis. Every inspection creates a verifiable performance record.
Multilingual Training
Training modules delivered in multiple languages so every team member can learn in the language they're most comfortable with. Completion tracked and verified automatically.
Performance Analytics
Real-time dashboards that track KPIs, SLA compliance, work order resolution, QA scores, and trend data. You see what we see — complete transparency into every metric that matters.
Robotics Integration
Autonomous cleaning equipment and robotic systems integrated into daily operations where they improve outcomes. Technology that supports people, not replaces them.
AI-Powered Auditing
AI validation layers that cross-reference inspection data, identify patterns, flag anomalies, and predict maintenance needs before they become problems.
Space Utilization
Data on how your spaces are actually being used — occupancy patterns, traffic flow, and usage frequency. This intelligence drives smarter cleaning schedules and resource allocation.

INTEGRATIONS

Connects With Your Existing Systems

AA360 doesn't replace your technology stack — it plugs into it. We integrate with the tools your team already relies on so you get a unified view without switching platforms.

  • TMA — Asset management and CMMS for MEP operations
  • Lighthouse — Real-time task completion tracking deployed at select accounts
  • Corrigo — Work order management and vendor coordination
  • Client Systems — Custom integrations with your existing facility management platforms
