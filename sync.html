SYNC Service Model

Task-based service delivery for clarity, consistency, and accountability

SERVICE DELIVERY

Every Role Defined. Every Task Assigned. Every Shift Accountable.

SYNC is A&A's task-based service model applied across all service lines. Instead of assigning generalists to cover everything, SYNC assigns 5 specialist roles — each with defined responsibilities, clear expectations, and measurable outputs.

The result is a service delivery structure that removes ambiguity, reduces gaps, and gives every team member a clear understanding of what they own. When people know exactly what's expected, they deliver consistently.

SYNC operates across janitorial, grounds maintenance, and MEP services, adapting to the specific needs of each facility while maintaining the same foundational accountability structure.

5 SPECIALIST ROLES

The SYNC Structure

Day Porter
Handles real-time facility needs during occupied hours — restroom checks, common area upkeep, spill response, and tenant requests. The visible face of daily service delivery.
Quality Inspector
Conducts scheduled QA audits, validates task completion, and documents performance against established benchmarks. Feeds data directly into AA360 for real-time reporting.
Project Specialist
Executes scheduled deep cleaning, floor care, carpet extraction, and other project-level work. Manages specialized equipment and ensures project tasks stay on cycle.
Shift Supervisor
Oversees nightly or shift-specific operations, manages team assignments, resolves on-site issues, and ensures every task list is completed before the shift closes out.
Account Manager
Your single point of contact. Manages client communication, coordinates across all SYNC roles, reviews performance data, and drives continuous improvement at the account level.

WHY SYNC WORKS

Built for Consistency, Designed for Accountability

Consistency
When every role has defined tasks and expectations, service quality doesn't fluctuate based on who's on shift. You get the same standard every day, every night, every building.
Accountability
Every task has an owner. If something gets missed, there's no confusion about who was responsible. That clarity drives pride and follow-through at every level of the team.
Scalability
SYNC scales from a single building to a multi-site portfolio. The same role structure applies whether you have 50 associates or 500, keeping operations predictable as your needs grow.

Clarity Drives Performance

SYNC replaces ambiguity with structure. If you're ready for a service model where every role, every task, and every shift is accounted for, let's talk about what SYNC looks like for your facility.
