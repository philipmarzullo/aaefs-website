Government

Certified, Compliant, and Accountable

Government facilities require contractors who meet strict compliance standards, maintain security protocols, and deliver transparent, documented performance. A&A is nationally certified as an MBE/MWBE, bringing both the credentials and the operational discipline that public-sector procurement teams require.

Our AA360 platform provides the reporting and audit trails government agencies need, while our SYNC service model ensures clear accountability at every level. We maintain the same performance standards across every facility we serve — because government buildings deserve the same excellence as any private-sector client.

Capabilities for Government

Security-Cleared Cleaning Teams
We can support background-checked and security-cleared teams for sensitive government environments. All personnel undergo thorough vetting processes aligned with your facility's security requirements.
Compliance & Documentation
Full documentation of service delivery, QA inspections, and performance metrics. AA360 generates the audit-ready reports that government procurement and oversight teams require for contract compliance verification.
Public Facility Standards
Government buildings serve the public and must reflect that responsibility. We maintain high-visibility areas, public restrooms, courthouses, municipal offices, and community spaces with the consistency and attention these environments demand.
MBE/MWBE Certified Services
A&A is nationally certified as a Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) and Minority/Women Business Enterprise (MWBE), supporting your agency's diversity procurement goals while delivering enterprise-grade facility services.
