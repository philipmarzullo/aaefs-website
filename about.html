Our Story

FOUNDED 1973 • YONKERS, NY

Built on Performance, Not Promises

A&A Elevated Facility Solutions was founded in 1973 with a simple idea: treat people right, and they'll take care of your buildings. More than 50 years later, that idea has grown into a company with 2,000+ employees, an employee-ownership model, and a reputation for keeping clients longer than anyone in the industry.

Under Armando Rodriguez Jr.'s leadership since 1985 — 41 years and counting — A&A has stayed true to the operator-first mentality that built the company. We don't compete on volume. We compete on excellence, accountability, and long-term relationships.

Today, A&A delivers integrated janitorial, grounds maintenance, and MEP services to commercial, institutional, and government clients. Our ESOP model means every employee has a stake in your building's success. Our technology platform, AA360, gives you real-time visibility into what's happening in your facility. And our 98%+ client retention rate proves that the model works.
