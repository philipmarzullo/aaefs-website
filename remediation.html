Remediation

Disaster recovery and environmental services through Armor Environmental

ARMOR ENVIRONMENTAL & WALLICO MAINTENANCE

When Disaster Strikes, We Respond

Emergencies don't wait for business hours. Through Armor Environmental, A&A's specialized remediation subsidiary, we provide rapid-response disaster recovery for water damage, fire and smoke events, and mold contamination. Our teams assess, contain, and restore — getting your facility back to safe, operational condition as fast as possible.

Wallico Maintenance, another A&A subsidiary, specializes in metal and stone restoration — from lobby marble and granite to exterior facades and architectural metalwork. Whether it's routine preservation or damage repair, Wallico brings decades of craft expertise to surfaces that define your building's character.

Together, Armor Environmental and Wallico Maintenance give A&A clients access to specialized recovery and restoration services without managing additional vendor relationships. It's all part of the integrated A&A model — one partner, full accountability.

Remediation & Restoration Capabilities

Water Damage Restoration
Emergency water extraction, structural drying, dehumidification, and moisture monitoring for floods, pipe bursts, and storm damage. Armor Environmental responds fast, documents everything, and restores your space to pre-loss condition.
Fire & Smoke Remediation
Soot removal, smoke odor elimination, structural cleaning, and content restoration after fire events. Our teams work with your insurance adjusters and restoration timeline to get affected areas cleaned, deodorized, and ready for rebuild or reoccupation.
Mold Remediation
Assessment, containment, removal, and prevention of mold contamination. Armor Environmental follows industry protocols for safe remediation — including air quality testing, HEPA filtration, and antimicrobial treatment — to protect occupant health and prevent recurrence.
Metal & Stone Restoration (Wallico)
Specialized maintenance and restoration of marble, granite, terrazzo, brass, stainless steel, and architectural metalwork. Wallico Maintenance preserves and restores the surfaces that define your building's aesthetic — from lobby floors to exterior facades.

Ready to Talk Performance?

Whether you're dealing with an active emergency or planning for prevention, A&A's remediation and restoration teams are ready. One call connects you to Armor Environmental and Wallico Maintenance — specialists backed by 50+ years of facility expertise.
