Post-Construction Cleaning

Dedicated cleaning teams for new builds and renovations

CONSTRUCTION-READY CLEANING

From Hard Hat to Move-In Ready

A&A maintains a dedicated post-construction cleaning team of 50+ staff available locally — trained, equipped, and ready to deploy on new builds, renovations, and tenant improvements. We understand that construction timelines are tight and punch lists don't wait.

Our post-construction crews work alongside general contractors and project managers to deliver rough cleans, detail cleans, and final cleans on schedule. We handle the dust, debris, adhesive residue, and construction film that stand between your project and occupancy.

Because we also deliver ongoing janitorial services, many clients bring us in for post-construction and keep us for the long term. That continuity means the team that cleans your building on day one already knows the space when they return on day two — and every day after.

Post-Construction Capabilities

Rough Clean
Initial debris removal after major construction phases — clearing dust, scrap materials, packaging, and bulk waste so trades can continue working in a clean environment. We coordinate with your GC to stay ahead of the schedule.
Detail Cleaning
Surface-by-surface deep cleaning of all finished areas — wiping fixtures, cleaning glass, removing adhesive residue, vacuuming HVAC vents, and scrubbing hard surfaces. Every detail addressed so the space is ready for final inspection.
Final Clean
The last pass before occupancy — a thorough, inspection-ready clean that addresses every surface, fixture, and corner. We clean to move-in standards so your space is ready for tenants, employees, or students on opening day.
Punch List Support
We work alongside your project team to address cleaning-related punch list items quickly and thoroughly. Touch-up cleans, spot treatments, and targeted corrections — whatever it takes to close out the list and hand over a finished space.

Ready to Talk Performance?

With 50+ dedicated post-construction staff available locally, A&A can deploy fast and deliver on your timeline. Whether it's a single-floor renovation or a multi-building campus project, let's talk about getting your space move-in ready.
