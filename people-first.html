People First™

Our operating philosophy. Not a slogan — the system that makes everything else work.

THE OPERATING SYSTEM

Dignity Drives Performance

People First™ is A&A's core operating philosophy. It's the belief that when people are treated with dignity, they treat others with respect and warmth. This creates energy, pride, and performance that no training manual can replicate.

In an industry where frontline workers are often invisible, A&A makes them the center of everything. We invest in their development, protect their safety, give them ownership through our ESOP, and build career paths that matter. The result: employees who care about your building as much as you do.

People First™ isn't something we talk about at orientation and forget. It drives hiring, training, scheduling, compensation, recognition, and how we show up for clients every single day.

How People First™ Shows Up

Training & Development
Multilingual training programs, APPA-level certification tracks, continuous learning through AA360. Every employee gets the tools to succeed.
Safety & Wellbeing
Comprehensive safety protocols, monthly safety awards, PPE compliance, and OSHA/HAZMAT training. We protect our people so they can protect your facility.
Ownership & Recognition
Through our ESOP, every A&A employee is a company owner. Combined with recognition programs and career advancement paths, this creates genuine pride in work.

CLIENT IMPACT

Why It Matters for Your Facility

People First™ isn't just good for employees — it's the engine behind A&A's 98%+ client retention rate. When frontline staff feel valued, they show up differently. They notice the details. They take initiative. They build relationships with your building's occupants.

The building services industry averages 200%+ annual turnover. A&A's People First™ approach significantly reduces that churn, which means your facility gets experienced, trained teams who know your building's rhythms and standards — not a revolving door of new faces every month.

  • Lower turnover means consistent service quality
  • Employee-owners are invested in outcomes, not just hours
  • Training investment compounds over time with stable teams
  • Strong culture attracts better talent in a competitive labor market
