Careers at A&A

Join an employee-owned company where your work matters

WORK WHERE YOU MATTER

Built on People First™

At A&A Elevated Facility Solutions, every team member is an owner. Our Employee Stock Ownership Plan (ESOP) means you have a direct stake in the company's success — and the company has a direct stake in yours.

Our People First™ philosophy isn't a recruitment tagline. It's how we operate every day. We invest in training, provide clear career paths, and treat every role — from frontline cleaning specialist to operations manager — with the dignity it deserves.

With 2,000+ employees across janitorial, grounds maintenance, and MEP operations, A&A offers diverse roles in a stable, growing company. Whether you're starting your career or bringing years of experience, there's a place for you here.

Why Work at A&A

Employee Ownership (ESOP)
Every team member is a company owner with a stake in our success. When you perform well, the company grows — and so does your ownership.
Training & Development
APPA-level certification, multilingual training manuals, continuous learning through AA360, green cleaning certification, and annual refresher training.
Safety & Wellbeing
Comprehensive safety programs, monthly safety awards, full PPE and OSHA compliance, and health protocols that protect you every day on the job.
Career Growth
Clear paths from frontline roles to supervision and management. We promote from within and invest in developing our people at every level.
Union Partnerships
25+ years of strong union relationships built on mutual respect. We value organized labor and work collaboratively with union partners.
Diverse Workplace
MBE/MWBE certified company with a commitment to dignity and inclusion. We serve diverse communities and our workforce reflects that.
