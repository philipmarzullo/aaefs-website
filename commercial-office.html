Commercial Office Space

Your Building's Reputation Starts at the Lobby

Tenant satisfaction depends on consistent, visible performance — clean lobbies, responsive maintenance, well-kept common areas. A&A delivers daily janitorial, MEP, and grounds services under one accountability structure so nothing falls between the cracks. Our manager-heavy model means someone from A&A is on-site every day verifying quality, not waiting for complaints to surface.

With SYNC defining clear roles and AA360 providing real-time reporting, property managers get the visibility they need to keep tenants confident in building operations.

Capabilities for Commercial Office

Daily Cleaning & Porter Services
Structured daily cleaning programs for lobbies, restrooms, common areas, and tenant floors. Porter services provide real-time responsiveness throughout the day so your building always looks its best.
MEP & Building Systems
Preventive maintenance for HVAC, electrical, plumbing, and fire/life safety systems. We use TMA for asset tracking and predictive scheduling, reducing unplanned downtime and extending equipment life.
Tenant Satisfaction Programs
Our People First™ culture means staff who represent your building with professionalism and warmth. We track satisfaction through regular QA cycles and adjust service delivery based on real feedback, not assumptions.
Green Cleaning Protocols
CIMS-GB certified programs using environmentally responsible products and practices. We help your building meet sustainability goals without compromising cleaning effectiveness or indoor air quality.
