Employee Ownership

An ESOP model that aligns every employee's success with yours.

EMPLOYEE STOCK OWNERSHIP PLAN

Every Employee Is an Owner

A&A is an Employee Stock Ownership Plan (ESOP) company. That means the person mopping your lobby, maintaining your HVAC system, or managing your grounds has a direct financial stake in the company's success — and yours.

ESOPs aren't common in the building services industry. Most competitors are private-equity owned or publicly traded, with incentives tied to quarterly returns. At A&A, our incentives are tied to long-term performance, client retention, and operational excellence — because that's what drives value for employee-owners.

The result is a workforce that thinks and acts like owners. They take initiative, reduce waste, protect your assets, and build real relationships with your building's occupants. That ownership mindset is the foundation of our 98%+ client retention rate.

What ESOP Means for Your Facility

Aligned Incentives
When the company performs well, employee-owners benefit directly. This creates natural alignment between workforce effort and client outcomes.
Lower Turnover
Employee ownership creates loyalty and long-term commitment. Less turnover means your facility gets experienced, trained teams who know your standards.
Accountability Culture
Owners don't cut corners. They protect assets, reduce waste, and take pride in their work because they have skin in the game.
