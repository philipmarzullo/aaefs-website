Life Sciences

Precision Environments Require Precision Maintenance

Life sciences facilities operate under strict environmental controls where even minor deviations can compromise research integrity or regulatory compliance. A&A brings experience with cleanroom environments, controlled spaces, and clinical research facilities — supported by documented QA processes and real-time reporting through AA360.

Our teams are trained on the specific protocols your facility requires, from gowning procedures to contamination prevention. Every cleaning event is logged, verified, and accessible to your compliance team.

Capabilities for Life Sciences

Controlled Environment Cleaning
Specialized cleaning protocols for cleanrooms, ISO-classified spaces, and controlled environments. Our teams follow strict gowning, chemical selection, and workflow procedures to maintain environmental integrity.
Compliance Documentation
Comprehensive documentation of every cleaning cycle, chemical application, and QA inspection. AA360 provides audit-ready records that support FDA, GMP, and institutional compliance requirements.
Specialized Equipment Maintenance
MEP support for HVAC systems, fume hoods, biosafety cabinets, and other critical equipment. Our TMA asset management system tracks maintenance schedules and ensures nothing falls through the cracks.
Research Lab Support
Dedicated cleaning and maintenance programs for research laboratories, including wet labs, dry labs, and shared instrumentation spaces. We understand the sensitivities of active research environments and staff accordingly.
